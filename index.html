The Fountain Festival

The Fountain Festival is an annual celebration to cap off the year and welcome in the new one.

So its kinda like New Years Eve right? Well yeah, but much better. NYE has lost its way, it sucks. The Fountain Festival is the answer.

The Fountain Festival isnt about resolutions and countdowns, its about celebrating the good times from the past year and bringing on the new year which promises to be even better.

No party poppers, no lines or overpriced and overrated nye events. Just getting together with friends, drinking from a fountain and having a good time.

No contamination of the Fountain.

Spilling your drink or similar liquid wastage is in direct opposition to the values of the fountain, and is therefore considered a fountain fail.

All fountain fails require the offending person to drink from the fountain immediately. If its due to spilling of a drink then amount spilled should be consumed.

Going to the bathroom is considered a non-sanctioned fountain and therefore the one true fountain should be honoured immediately by consuming from it.
